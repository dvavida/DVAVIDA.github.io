Pharaoh Soldiers | Solar Dates

Pharaoh statues are not mystical kings, they are astronomical devices for determining the sun's trajectory. The true name is FARAON - the lamp is on. Two global astronomical events align on the same street in perspective. First, in March at sunrise on the east side, men’s summer begins. Then, on September 1st at sunset, men’s summer ends and women’s summer begins on that same street. Pharaoh - FARA ON/OFF light (sun-bulb). The sides of the Pharaoh’s head is receding streets in perspective, and horizontal strips (SOLDIERS – solar dates) mark sunrise and sunset on precise dates.
